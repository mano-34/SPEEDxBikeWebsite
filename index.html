About us

Stunt motorbiking, also known as motorcycle stunt riding, is a motorsport involving performing various acrobatic and technical maneuvers on a motorcycle. These tricks include wheelies, stoppies, drifting, and jumps, often executed in a freestyle or competition setting. Specialized motorcycles, often heavily modified, are used to perform these stunts, with an emphasis on rider ergonomics and control.
